From Vision to Reality

01. Consultation & Floor Plan

  • In-depth discussion to understand your goals, lifestyle, and budget.
  • Site analysis to evaluate opportunities and constraints.
  • Development of initial floor plans and spatial layouts.

02. Schematic Design & Visualization

  • Creation of detailed architectural drawings.
  • Development of 3D models and photorealistic renderings.
  • Selection of preliminary materials and finishes.

03. Review & Refinement

  • Collaborative review sessions to gather your feedback.
  • Refinement of designs to align with your vision and budget.
  • Finalization of construction documents and specifications.

04. Construction & Handover

  • Overseeing the construction phase to ensure quality standards.
  • Regular site visits and progress reports.
  • Final inspection and handover of your completed project.
